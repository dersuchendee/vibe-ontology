Aestheticc back to ToC or Class ToC

IRI: https://w3id.org/vibe-ontology/Aesthetic

A culturally emergent, open-ended category that groups a collection of heterogeneous elements (e.g., images, colors, objects, sounds, texts) perceived as coherently expressing a recognizable style, affect, or orientation. It is not defined by necessary and sufficient conditions, but by shared associations and family resemblances established by a community. E.g, the Frutiger Aero aesthetic groups glossy textures, bright gradients, nature imagery, and optimistic future cues into a recognizable early-2000s style.
