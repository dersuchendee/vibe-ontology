Abstract

The Vibe Ontology is a multilingual ontology that models aesthetics as emergent, collection-based, and culturally situated open-ended categories grounded in representative associations. The ontology is aligned with DOLCE Zero and SKOS, modelling aesthetics as first-class entities whose nature is captured through two core mechanisms: *representativeness*, encoding culturally shared but non-exhaustive associations with entities such as motifs, values, and platforms; and *collection*, capturing the structure through which aesthetics emerge from heterogeneous sets of elements.
